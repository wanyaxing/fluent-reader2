# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

**最后更新：2024年12月31日**

## 简介

MF RSS Reader 是一款浏览器扩展，用于订阅和阅读 RSS/Atom 源。我们非常重视您的隐私。

## 数据收集

**本扩展不收集、存储或传输任何个人信息到我们的服务器。**

所有数据（包括您的 RSS 订阅列表、文章阅读状态、设置偏好）均存储在您的浏览器本地存储中，不会发送到任何外部服务器。

## AI 功能说明

如果您启用了 AI 摘要或翻译功能，您的文章内容将发送到您自行配置的第三方 AI 服务（如 OpenAI 或兼容服务）。这些请求直接从您的浏览器发送到您配置的 API 端点，我们不会经手或存储这些数据。请参阅您所使用的 AI 服务提供商的隐私政策。

## 网络请求

本扩展会向以下地址发起网络请求：
- 您订阅的 RSS/Atom 源地址（用于获取文章内容）
- 您配置的 AI API 服务地址（仅在启用 AI 功能时）

## 权限使用

- **storage / unlimitedStorage**: 在本地存储您的订阅和设置
- **alarms**: 定时刷新订阅源
- **host_permissions**: 获取各网站的 RSS 源内容

## 联系方式

如有任何隐私相关问题，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系我们。

# Privacy Policy (English)

**Last Updated: December 31, 2024**

## Introduction

MF RSS Reader is a browser extension for subscribing to and reading RSS/Atom feeds. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection

**This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.**

All data (including your RSS subscriptions, reading status, and preferences) is stored locally in your browser and is never sent to any external servers.

## AI Features

If you enable AI summary or translation features, your article content will be sent to third-party AI services (such as OpenAI or compatible services) that you configure yourself. These requests are sent directly from your browser to your configured API endpoint. We do not handle or store this data. Please refer to the privacy policy of your chosen AI service provider.

## Network Requests

This extension makes network requests to:
- RSS/Atom feed URLs you subscribe to (to fetch article content)
- AI API service URLs you configure (only when AI features are enabled)

## Permission Usage

- **storage / unlimitedStorage**: Store your subscriptions and settings locally
- **alarms**: Periodically refresh your subscriptions
- **host_permissions**: Fetch RSS feed content from various websites

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us through GitHub Issues.
